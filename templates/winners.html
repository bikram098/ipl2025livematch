IPL Timeline
2008 - The Beginning

The IPL was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2007. The first season began on April 18, 2008, with Rajasthan Royals becoming the inaugural champions.

2011 - Expansion to 10 Teams

The IPL expanded to 10 teams with the addition of Pune Warriors India and Kochi Tuskers Kerala, though Kochi was terminated the following year.

2013 - The Spot-Fixing Controversy

The 2013 season was marred by spot-fixing allegations, leading to lifetime bans for several players and the suspension of two franchises.

2016 - Record-Breaking Season

Virat Kohli scored a record 973 runs in a single season, including four centuries, as RCB finished runners-up to Sunrisers Hyderabad.

2020 - Pandemic Edition

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the entire tournament was held in the UAE with Mumbai Indians winning their fifth title.

2022 - Mega Auction & Expanded League

The IPL expanded to 10 teams again with the addition of Gujarat Titans and Lucknow Super Giants. Gujarat Titans won in their debut season.

2023 - Chennai's Comeback

Chennai Super Kings won their fifth IPL title, equaling Mumbai Indians' record, with MS Dhoni leading the team at the age of 41.
